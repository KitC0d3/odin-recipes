Recipes

Ingredients Needed:

  • 500 g chicken pieces (drumsticks, wings, or thighs)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup milk or water
  • 1 tsp paprika or chili powder (optional for color/spice)
  • A little oil or butter

Steps:

  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken pieces.
  2. Dip each chicken piece in the egg mixture, then coat it evenly with flour.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan on medium heat.
  4. Fry chicken pieces in batches for 8–12 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve hot with ketchup, chili sauce, or gravy.
