Recipes

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 chicken breast (or 2 thighs)
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A little oil or butter

Steps:

  1. Wash and cut the chicken and vegetables.
  2. Heat a little oil or butter in a pot.
  3. Add chicken pieces and stir for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in water or broth, add carrots, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then lower heat. Simmer for 15–20 minutes until chicken and carrots are soft.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning.
